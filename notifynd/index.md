# Privacy Policy — NotiFynd

**Last updated:** 10 May 2026
**App:** NotiFynd (Android)
**Developer:** Kakoo Apps
**Contact:** kakooapps@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information the NotiFynd Android app
("the app", "we", "us") collects, why it is collected, how it is used,
and the choices you have. By installing or using NotiFynd you agree to
the practices described below.

---

## 1. Information we collect

NotiFynd is a notification recovery app. It requires Notification Access
permission to read and store your device notifications locally.

### Data stored locally on your device

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Notification content (app name, title, body, timestamp, icon) | Display and search notifications you've received or dismissed | Room database (private app storage) |
| OTP codes extracted from notifications | Quick access to one-time passwords | Room database (private app storage) |
| App preferences (blocked apps, theme, retention period) | User settings | DataStore (private app storage) |

**All notification data is stored locally on your device only.** It is never
uploaded to our servers or any third party. Data is removed when you
uninstall the app.

### Data collected by third-party services

| Service | Provider | Data collected | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Google LLC | Advertising ID, IP address, coarse device/OS info, ad-interaction events | Serve and measure ads |
| Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) | Google LLC | Consent choices, region | Comply with GDPR / CCPA / state privacy laws |
| Firebase Analytics | Google LLC | App-usage events, pseudonymous app-instance ID, coarse device/OS info | Understand aggregate usage to improve the app |
| Firebase Crashlytics | Google LLC | Crash stack traces, device model, OS version, app version | Diagnose and fix crashes |

We do **not** collect or store:
- Your name, email, phone number, address, or any other contact details
- Photos, contacts, calendars, microphone audio, or precise location
- Notification content on any remote server

---

## 2. Notification Access permission

NotiFynd requires the **Notification Access** (NotificationListenerService)
permission to function. This permission allows the app to:
- Read incoming notifications to store them locally
- Detect when notifications are dismissed to enable recovery

You can revoke this permission at any time in **Settings → Apps → Special
app access → Notification access**. Without this permission, NotiFynd
cannot capture or recover notifications.

---

## 3. How we use the information

- **Local notification storage** is used solely to let you search, browse,
  and recover notifications on your device.
- **AdMob** uses data to serve ads — personalized where you have consented,
  non-personalized otherwise.
- **Firebase Analytics** is used in aggregate form only to understand usage
  patterns.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** uses crash data to fix bugs.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties
beyond the providers listed above.

---

## 4. Data retention & deletion

- Free users: notifications are automatically deleted after **5 days**.
- Premium users: **unlimited** retention — notifications are never
  auto-deleted.
- You can manually delete individual notifications or bulk-delete via
  multi-select.
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently.

## 5. Children's privacy

NotiFynd is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children. The app targets users
aged 13 and above.
