# Delete Your Data — Catchyu

**App:** Catchyu - Auto Responder
**Developer:** KaKooApps

---

## How to Delete Your Data

All data in Catchyu is stored locally on your device. If you use the optional Google Drive Backup feature (Pro only), a copy of your data is stored in your personal Google Drive account. You have full control over your data at all times.

### Delete Individual Records

1. **Leads:** Open a lead → tap the delete icon → confirm deletion
2. **Rules:** Open a rule → tap delete → confirm deletion
3. **Templates:** Open a template → tap delete → confirm deletion
4. **Notes:** Open a note → tap delete → confirm deletion
5. **Blocked Contacts:** Go to Settings → Blocked Contacts → tap unblock

### Delete All Data at Once

1. Open Settings
2. Use the **Clear All** actions to remove all leads, rules, templates, or notes

### Delete Google Drive Backup

If you used the optional Backup feature (Pro only), your data is stored in a hidden app folder in your Google Drive. To delete it:

1. **From the app:** Open Settings → Backup → **Sign Out** — this disconnects your Google account from the app
2. **From Google:** Visit [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) → find **Catchyu** → click **Remove Access** — this revokes the app's access and removes the hidden app data folder from your Drive

### Delete Everything (Full Reset)

1. Go to **Android Settings → Apps → Catchyu**
2. Tap **Clear Data** — this removes all app data including preferences
3. Or simply **Uninstall** the app — all local data is permanently deleted

**Note:** Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your Google Drive backup. To remove the backup, sign out from the app first or revoke access at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

## Delete Your Account

Catchyu uses **Google Sign-In** solely for the optional Google Drive Backup feature. No account is created on our servers.

To remove the Google Sign-In association:

1. **From the app:** Open Settings → Backup → **Sign Out**
2. **From Google:** Visit [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) → find **Catchyu** → click **Remove Access**

This will:
- Revoke Catchyu's access to your Google Drive
- Remove the hidden app data folder containing your backup
- Disconnect your Google account from the app

## Data NOT Stored by Us

- We do not operate any server that stores your data
- Google Sign-In is used only for Drive backup access — no account is created on our servers
- If you used the optional Backup feature, your data is stored in your own Google Drive account's hidden app folder. Sign out from the app or revoke access at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) to remove it
- Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collect only anonymous usage and crash data

## Third-Party Data

If you enabled optional features, contact the providers directly:

- **Google Drive:** Visit [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) to manage app access

- **Google Gemini:** https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13278668
